Blueprint to Success

  • Compatability Scoring

    This allows Primes and Diverse Suppliers to view their compatibility score according to the opportunities posted by the Prime.

  • Ability to schedule meetings virtually

    Primes are able to schedule and meet virtually with Diverse Suppliers avoiding the hassle and cost of travel.

  • Post Opportunities

    This tool provides Primes the ability to post current or upcoming opportunities, along with the requirements and the value.

  • Filter Suppliers

    The tool is able to filter Diverse Suppliers by any specific Business Category or attribute.

  • Rate suppliers

    Allows Primes to rate Diverse Suppliers according to the first initial meeting and through the follow up process.

About

Puerto Rico-Open For Business is a multifaceted tool that is investing in the growth and expansion of Puerto Rican Owned and Operated Businesses. Working with the Puerto Rican Government In an effort to provide a tool that allows local Vendors to connect with various government agencies, corporations, and International entities to help increase their range in the business world. Our user will be able to understand and evaluate various business opportunities on a scale never seen before.
